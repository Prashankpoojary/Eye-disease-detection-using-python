About Us

Meet the Team Behind I.R.I.S We are a dedicated team of four aspiring engineers from Sahyadri College of Engineering and Management, pursuing our Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. Under the guidance of our mentor, Mr. Naveen Kumar, we have embarked on a journey to leverage the power of Machine Learning for the early detection of eye diseases. Project Details: Our project, titled I.R.I.S (Intelligent Retinal Imaging System), focuses on the early detection of eye diseases such as Cataract, Glaucoma, and Diabetic Retinopathy using advanced Machine Learning algorithms. By harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence, we aim to revolutionize the field of ophthalmology and make a meaningful impact on healthcare. Join us on this journey as we strive to make a difference in the lives of individuals affected by eye diseases through innovation and technology.
